What is FOSS?

  • FOSS stands for Free and Open Source Software
  • Actually 2 Terms : Free Software + Open Source
  • Source Code is openly Shared
  • No Restrictions on
  • 1>Using
  • 2>Studying
  • 3>Changing

History of FOSS and Mozilla

  • "Free Software" Philosophy - Mr. Richard Stallman
  • "Open Source" - Mr. Eric Raymond & Mr. Bruce Perens
  • The Cathedral and the Bazaar - Mr. Eric Raymond
  • Netscape Goes Open Source -> Mozilla Firefox

So what exactly is Mozilla?

Mozilla is…

a global community of people who believe that openness, innovation, and opportunity are key to the continued health of the Internet.
